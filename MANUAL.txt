----------------------------------Commands-------------------------------------
calserv [ACTION]: Put an action here.
  READ [MONTH]: Put a month to read.
    ANYMONTH [DAY]: Enter day to display.
      1-31: Show all events for that day.
      0: Show the main events for every applicable day in the month.

Example: [READ NOV 7] would show the list of reminders for The 7th of November
